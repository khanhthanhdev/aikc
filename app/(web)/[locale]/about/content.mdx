## What is {config.site.name}?

{config.site.name} is a **free, curated collection of AI tools designed for education and productivity**. Browse our comprehensive directory and discover AI-powered recommendations that help you excel in your work and studies.

From coding and programming, to content creation, image generation, writing and editing, presentations, mathematics, language learning, and beyond – {config.site.name} aggregates the **best AI tools available** to support your learning and productivity journey. Whether you're a student, educator, professional, or lifelong learner, you'll find practical AI solutions designed to help you succeed in various areas of education and skill development.

{config.site.name} is more than just a directory; it's **a community-driven knowledge base**. Explore curated tools, read detailed reviews, and get personalized recommendations. We believe in the power of AI to transform education and empower learners worldwide.

## Our Mission

We're dedicated to making quality AI tools accessible to everyone, regardless of background or experience level. By curating and reviewing the best tools available, we help learners and professionals make informed decisions and maximize their productivity.
